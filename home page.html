Summer Semester 2021-2022

MidTerm

Final Exam

Final exams will start on Thursday January 13, 2022 according to the attached schedule and will end on Thursday February 3, 2022. Kindly check the dates and time slots for your respective exams. Please note that the exam for the Creative Thinking and Communication Skills course to be held on Tuesday January 18, 2022 will be for one hour only. The first level class will be divided into two groups; the first group will take the exam from 9:00 AM till 10:00 AM, while the second group will take the exam from 10:30 AM till 11:30 AM. Therefore the exams for the second slot on that day will be from 12:30 PM till 2:30 PM. Good luck to all of you.
